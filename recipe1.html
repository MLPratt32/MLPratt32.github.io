Herb Stuffed Branzino

Ingredients

  • 2 (1lb) whole branzino
  • 5 Tbs of olive oil
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 7 sprigs of rosemary
  • 4 pinches of salt
  • 3 pinches of ground pepper
  • 2 whole lemons, sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Put fish on your tray and massage the inside and outside of the fish with Olive Oil. Then rub in your salt and pepper.
  3. Cut lemons into thin slices, and place inside the fish with rosemary sprigs and minced garlic.
  4. Bake 12-14 minutes, then turn on the broiler and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Remove when the fish begins to flake when moved.
  6. Enjoy!
