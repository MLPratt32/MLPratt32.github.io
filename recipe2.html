"Tandoori" Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 1lb of chicken wings
  • 1 tbs of olive oil
  • 3 cups of greek yogurt
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tbs of ground or minced ginger
  • 1 tbs of honey
  • 3 pinches of salt
  • 3 pinches of ground pepper
  • 1 lemon, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbs of garam masala
  • 2 tbs of coriander
  • 1 tbs of cumin
  • 1 tsp of cayenne
  • 2 bird's eye chilis, diced
  • 3 tbs of soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Create the marinade by mixing together:
    • yogurt
    • honey
    • garlic
    • ginger
    • salt
    • ground pepper
    • garam masala
    • cayenne
    • cumin
    • coriander
    • bird's eye chilis
    • soy sauce
  2. Cover chicken wings with marinade and refrigerate for an hour and a half.
  3. Preheat oven to 375.
  4. Spray pan with a hint of olive oil, to prevent sticking
  5. Bake for 45 minutes. Then turn the oven onto broil for three minutes. The top of the chicken should char, creating a lovely barbeque flavour.
  6. Remove when the chicken wings reach 165 degrees F.
  7. Enjoy!
