Mira's Spiced Turkey Chili

Ingredients

  • 2lbs of ground turkey
  • 2 cups of chicken stock
  • 8 ounces of canned, diced tomato
  • 1 can of garbanzo beans
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 2 jalapenos
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 yellow onion, large
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 3 pinches of salt
  • 3 pinches of ground pepper
  • 2 tbs of garam masala
  • 2 tbs of coriander
  • 1 tbs of cumin
  • 2 tsp of cayenne
  • 1 tsp of cinamon
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 tbs of chili powder
  • 2 tbs of smoked paprika
  • 4 tbs of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Begin by dicing all of your vegetables. Pour 2 tbs of olive oil into your pot, and turn the flame to medium high heat. Then, begin by cooking your onions, garlic, and dry spices until the onions are transparent, about 4-6 minutes. Then add in your mushrooms, jalapenos, and bell peppers and cook for another 4 minutes.
  2. Once the vegetables have softened, add your ground turkey to the pot. Cook until the trukey has begun to separate into small round chunks and has browned.
  3. Once the turkey has browned, mix in your canned diced tomatoes. Let cook for 5 minutes. While this begins to simmer, drain and rinse your beans.
  4. Mix in your chicken stock, garbanzo beans, and bay leaves. Bring your chili mixture to a boil, beofre lowering the flame to a simmer. Cover pot, turn and let in cook down for 45 minutes to an hour. Return ever 10 to 15 minutes to stir to prevent the chili from sticking to the bottom on the pot.
  5. Take the chili off the heat, serve, and enjoy!
