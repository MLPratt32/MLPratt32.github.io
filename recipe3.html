Prosciutto and Vegetable Tortellini

Ingredients

  • 1 package of prosciutto
  • 2 shallots
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 2 tbs of olive oil
  • 1 bag of tortellini
  • 3 pinches of salt
  • 3 pinches of ground pepper
  • 1/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Begin by bringing a large pot of water to a boil. While the water is boiling, begin preparing the rest of your ingredients.
  2. Mice your shallots, garlic, mushrooms, and prosciutto into small cubes.
  3. Pour 1 tbs of olive oil into frying pan and raise the flame up to medium. Once oil is heated, place onions and garlic in the pan. Cook until the onions are transparent.
  4. While the onions are cooking, your water should have begun to boil. Put tortellini in the water and cook until all of the pieces have risen to the surface of the water. This should take 3-4 minutes. Drain pasta and set to the side.
  5. Turn up the flame to medium-high heat and place the mushrooms and prosciutto into the pan. Cook for 3 minutes, then add in the remaining olive oil and tortellini.
  6. Cook until the pasta and prosciutto have crisped. Pour in your frozen peas, and sprinkle in your parmesan cheese. Cook for another minute or until the peas have cooked.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
